How It Works

Our system is designed to be simple yet effective, improving the experience for both citizens and staff.

Book Appointment

Citizens can book their Aadhaar service appointment through the mobile app, website, or via SMS.

Receive Confirmation

An SMS confirmation with queue number, estimated time, and document checklist is sent immediately.

Get Reminders

Timely notifications are sent as the appointment time approaches.

Arrive at Center

Citizens arrive at the center close to their appointment time, reducing waiting periods.

Document Verification

Quick verification of documents ensures all requirements are met before proceeding.

Service Completion

Efficient processing of Aadhaar services with minimal wait time.
